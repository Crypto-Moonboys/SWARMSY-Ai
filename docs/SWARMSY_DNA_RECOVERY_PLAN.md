# SWARMSY DNA Recovery Plan

## Purpose

Prevent loss of important product identity while moving from earlier SWARMSY/Graffiti Kings experiments into the AnythingLLM-based architecture.

Sources reviewed:

- GRAFFITI-KINGS-APP
- DIZ-A-REMIX
- SWARMSY-Ai direction

The goal is not to copy old implementations. The goal is to preserve the ideas that make SWARMSY unique.

## Architecture Rule

Do not rebuild the AI brain.

```
AnythingLLM = AI engine and workspace infrastructure
SPARKY = identity guide and behaviour layer
SWARMSY = mission, memory, packs and creator operating system
```

## Preserve

- identity building
- creator memory
- Creative DNA
- workspace knowledge
- mission-driven AI behaviour
- user ownership of decisions
- continuity between sessions

## Remove From Core

Do not bring forward prematurely:

- custom AI infrastructure
- full autonomous swarm systems
- complex sync systems
- token/reward mechanics
- platform replacement work

The first proof is whether one creator becomes more effective.
